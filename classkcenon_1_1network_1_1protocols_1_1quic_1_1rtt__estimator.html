Detailed Description

RTT estimation for QUIC (RFC 9002 Section 5)

Implements the RTT estimation algorithm as specified in RFC 9002. Tracks smoothed RTT, RTT variance, minimum RTT, and calculates the probe timeout (PTO) duration.
